Thank you for opening an account with MarketsWorld.
If you'd like some pointers getting started, visit our step-by-step guide here. You are free to deposit and start trading as soon as you like and benefit from up to 100% returns per trade.
Please note when you make your first deposit there will be a temporary hold on your ability to withdraw which will be removed once your account is fully verified. To do this we ask that you provide:
Proof of Identity. The best identification documents are those which are issued by a government authority, which should have your photograph, address and signature. For example a passport, election identity card, driver's license, etc. would be sufficient for the purpose of establishing identity.
Proof of Address. This is a document to establish your home address, which can be either a recent utility bill, a bank or credit card statement, or other official written correspondence addressed to you at your home and dated within the last three months.
Copies of all cards used for deposit. Please send a copy of the front of all the cards you have used to deposit with us, blocking the middle digits so that only the first 6 and final 4 numbers are visible.
Please email these documents to Verification@e3.marketsworldstaging.com. Once these documents are received and your account is verified we will process any withdrawal requests that you make within 24hours.
To ensure you always receive your withdrawal confirmations and news on new developments such as additional expiry times and trading assets, please add Verification@e3.marketsworldstaging.com and Service@e3.marketsworldstaging.com to your "safe senders" or whitelist.
